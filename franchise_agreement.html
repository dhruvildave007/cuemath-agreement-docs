  1. develop, design, operate and maintain:
  1. its proprietary “Cuemath Learning System” or “Tools and Materials” comprising of (a) workbook(s)/ worksheet(s), (b) its proprietary the “Cuestudent”, “Cueteacher”, “Cueparent” App and such other Apps developed and deployed in future; (c) website (d) mathematical puzzle cards; (e) its proprietary “Cuestudent”, “Cueteacher”, “Cueparent” Apps and such other Apps developed and deployed in future, and web application(s); (f) a Physical mathematical model kit (Math Box); (all of the above being referred to as the “System”); and (g) any other components, application or data, that may be added to the System from time to time;
  2. its proprietary “Cuemath Curriculum” (hereinafter referred to as “Curriculum”); and
  3. its proprietary “Cuestudent”, “Cueteacher”, “Cueparent” Apps and such other Apps developed and deployed in future, (hereinafter referred to as “App”);
  1. design all promotional materials that may be required by the Franchisee to promote the Cuemath Learning Program (all such materials being hereinafter referred to as “Promotion Materials”);
  1. provide the Franchisee with a secure login to the System, so as to enable the Franchisee (i) to track prospective student leads; and (ii) to record all such data as may be required by the Company, including for the purposes of maintaining the most current data pertaining to each Enrolled Student, to track the performance of the Enrolled Students, and for training and assessment purposes of either Enrolled Students or Franchisee;
  1. provide the Franchisee with a format of a Fee Invoice/ Receipt, link or web application to enable collection of Fees from Parents (on behalf of Enrolled Students) in accordance with Annexure A of this Agreement and provide details of designated bank account for benefits of Franchisee;

  1. Ensure compliance with the Consideration and Payment Mechanism laid out in Annexure A.


  1. 5. Obligations of the Franchisee : The Franchisee shall at all times during the Term:
  1. Enroll no more than 6 (Six) students per batch to whom he/she will deliver the Teaching Sessions;
  2. Run the Cuemath Program for Enrolled Students and will not teach any subject offered by the Company to other students, who are not Enrolled Students.
  3. Maintain regular orientation with the Cuemath Program and updates to the same;
  4. Attend all certification programmes and training programmes mandated by the Company from time to time, and shall be fully acquainted with, and equipped to, use the Cuemath Program, as well as being acquainted with the manner in which the Company expects the Curriculum to be delivered to the Enrolled Students;
  5. Wherever applicable, maintain and display prominently at the Learning Centre, the registration certificate issued to him/ her by the Company upon completion of the “Franchisee Evaluation Process”;
  6. Adhere to the Company’s Code of Conduct, Data Privacy Policy and Other Policies as applicable from time to time, and also the Company ’s stated values and principles, policies as notified on Company’s website;
  7. Adhere to the highest standards of ethical conduct in his/ her dealings and transactions with the Company and the Parents, including (i) offering the same fee structure to the Enrolled Students as has been discussed and agreed by the Company and the Franchisee, which fee structure is based on the demand assessed for the Teaching Sessions, as well as other relevant commercial factors in the locality of the Cuemath Learning Centre; and (ii) adhering to the Company’s policy on grant or non-grant of any discounts to the Enrolled Students (iii) agree with the enrolled, students and parents the details of timing of student classes, grades and learning objectives;
  8. Procure at his/ her expense from Company or its pre-notified and certified vendor, Set Up Kit, promotional material to deliver the Teaching Sessions, as developed by the Company. The Franchisee should purchase his/her own tablet device/ laptop, as the case may be, of recommended specifications and operating system specified by the Company.
  9. Ensure uninterrupted internet connectivity on tablet device, so as to be able to deliver the Teaching Sessions effectively;
  10. Maintain the Franchisee Learning Centre in a clean and hygienic condition at all times while rendering the Teaching Sessions, wherever applicable;
  11. Be well groomed and behave in a manner that is appropriate for becoming of a Teacher;
  12. Not consume any tobacco products or alcohol or any other illegal substance during a Teaching Session;
  13. Ensure that he/she shall have with him/her, a functional mobile phone with a valid sim card (bearing the same mobile number that she has registered with the Company at the Effective Date) (“Registered Mobile Number”) at all times, particularly during a Teaching Session;
  14. Maintain the Registered Mobile Number during the Term. Any change in the mobile number should be duly intimated to the Company Personnel within 1 (one) day of obtaining the new number;
  15. Ensure that he/she provides access to the Cuemath Learning Centre being run and operated by him/ her, wherever applicable, to any of the Company’s Representatives or third parties authorised by the Company, upon the Company providing him/ her with reasonable advance notice;
  16. Contact the designated personnel of the Company in the event she encounters any issue or on occurrence of any incident/ accident whatsoever while rendering the Teaching Sessions;
  17. Ensure that the Data, Materials and other items used by the Franchisee for the conduct of the Teaching Sessions do not bear any trademarks, logos, stickers, graphics or images apart from those belonging to the Company;
  18. Collect and maintain information in respect of each Enrolled Student immediately upon enrolling a student, including but not limited to the Enrolled Student’s name, school, grade, home address, Parent's name, Parent's number, exam results, Olympiad score, school events, reason for joining Cuemath, and any other information as may be required by the Company and provide such information to the Company immediately thereafter and also as and when requested by the Company in digital form all such information been collected and collated shall be called as ‘Student Data’ and shall be maintained by Company;
  19. Ensure that Franchisee does not assign his/her obligation hereto to any third person to deliver the Teaching Sessions to the Enrolled Students;
  20. Ensure that the Teaching Sessions are conducted only within the designated classroom of the Cuemath Learning Centre or digitally, as the case may be, and nowhere else.
  21. Strictly adhere to: (i) all Company Policies; and (ii) provisions of Applicable Law while conducting the Teaching Sessions and complies with all tax liabilities and obligations in relation to Franchise.
  22. Approach the Company directly in the event he/she has to report any grievances, complaints or issues that he/she may have encountered/suffered in relation to the Cuemath Program. Under no circumstances shall the Franchisee directly or indirectly report any such grievances, complaints or issues in relation to the Cuemath Program to the Parent or publicise any such grievances, complaints or issues;
  23. Do not do anything or make any statement in any media or to third person whosoever that can cause harm to the Company’s brand, reputation or business;
  24. Maintain strict confidentiality of the Student Data and other information belonging to the Company, and unless expressly authorized by the Company, shall not share such details to any third party. The Franchisee agrees and acknowledges that he/she shall be solely responsible for any breach of the above, whether or not the same have been authorized by him/her; and
  25. In the event any accident is caused by the Franchisee which results in any injury/disability/ death being caused to the Enrolled Students, the Franchisee shall be solely responsible and liable to indemnify the Company, against any and all claims that may be made by the Parents and other third party on the Company and/or its officers, directors, agents, employees, affiliates and/or representatives in respect of any such injury/ disability/death.
  26. Inform the Company at least 7 days in advance if Franchisee plans to remain absent from the centre for more than 3 (three) consecutive days.
  27. Undertake and ensure that (i) no resident or worker or employee or person working or employed at the Franchisee’s premises (including the Cuemath Learning Centre, wherever applicable) (“Learning Centre Personnel”) have any criminal record and/or have been involved in any objectionable, criminal or untoward incidents in the Franchisee premises (including the Cuemath Learning Centre, wherever applicable) and /or (ii) such Learning Centre Personnel are at all times, under the supervision of and are the solely the responsibility of Franchisee. It is clarified that at no time or occasion shall the Company be responsible or liable for any acts, omissions committed by or on account of the Learning Centre Personnel and the Franchisee shall keep the Company, its officers, employees, representatives and agents indemnified for any claims, losses and /or damages claimed against the Company as a result of any such act or omissions (criminal or otherwise) committed by the Learning Centre Personnel.
